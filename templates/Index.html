Overview


This site examines Billboard Hot 100 charts from 1958 until the current year and analyzes the lyrics and artists of the top songs to determine trends.   Data was obtained by extracting song titles and lyrics, weeks spent at number 1 and artists.   Below are brief descriptions of the key dimensions of our analysis.   To further analyze any area, click on the relevant area of analysis below.   Alternately, you can click on any item in the menu bar at the top of the page.



Summary of Visualizations




Historical Effects on Lyrics - Analysis of whether or not major world events
    had any effect on the lyrics of the top songs throughout the years.
Songs & Lyrics - Sentiment analysis of the lyrics in top songs to determine
    similarities between the lyrics of top songs. throughout the years.
Artists - Analysis of artists data to determine the profile of artistes with
    number one songs.
Overview of 60yrs of music charts
Probabilistic Lyric Generator - Based on all the data analyzed, machine learning
    techniques were used to generate/predict the lyrics of typical songs for each decade.
